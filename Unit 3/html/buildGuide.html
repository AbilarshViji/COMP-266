How to Build a PC

Parts Required

  • CPU
  • CPU Cooler
  • Motherboard
  • RAM
  • Storage
  • GPU (Graphics Card)
  • Case
  • Power Supply

Motherboard

The motherboard is where your CPU, CPU cooler, RAM, M.2 SSDs, GPU, and case connectors will connect to.
Begin by placing the motherboard on a non-conductive surface. Many of the steps below will involve the motherboard.

CPU

Now is time to install your CPU into the motherboard

  1. Undo the retention arm on the motherboards CPU socket
  2. Place the CPU in the socket, ensuring the triangle on the CPU matches with the triangle on the motherboard
  3. Place the retention arm back in place

CPU Cooler

Now is time to install the CPU cooler

  1. Remove the plastic cover on the bottom of the heat sink
  2. Line up the holes on the CPU cooler with the holes on the motherboard
  3. Screw the CPU cooler in

RAM

RAM is very simple to install, just undo the clip on the connector on the motherboard and push the ram in. Ensure that the RAM is oriented correctly

Power Supply

The power supply or PSU is also fairly simple to install. Just screw the PSU into the case, and preroute the wires.

Storage

If you have SATA storage devices, place the devices in the case, and connect the power and data connectors
If you have a M.2 storage device, find the connector on your motherboard, and screw the drive in.

Case

Now is the time to place the motherboard into the case

  1. Place the IO shield into the case. Ensure it is oriented correctly
  2. Place the motherboard into the case, lining it up with the ports in the IO shield and holes in the case
  3. Screw the case into the motherboard
  4. Connect the front panel connectors from the case to the motherboard
  5. Connect the power cables to the motherboard
  6. Connect the SATA cables from the SATA drives to the motherboard

GPU (Graphics Card)

Now its time to connect the GPU

  1. Unclip the PCIe clip
  2. Remove the PCIe covers in the case
  3. Connect the GPU to the motherboard, it should click in
  4. Screw the GPU to the case
