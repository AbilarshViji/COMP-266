Power Supply (PSU)

The power supply powers all the components of the computer. There are 2 form factors of PSUs: SFX and ATX. SFX is meant for small form factor cases. Your case will let you know if you need a SFX power supply. The connectors that connect the PSU to the motherboard and other components of the computer have been standardized for a long time, so there will be no issues with this. You can use the power supply calculator to determine how much power your PC will need.
