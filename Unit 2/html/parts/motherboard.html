Motherboard

The motherboard is where all the components will connect to, and allow for communication between all the components. The CPU, RAM, storage, GPU, and power supply all connect to the motherboard. Other smaller connectors, such as front panel USB/audio and case fans also connect to the motherboard. Motherboards come in various sizes: Mini-ITX, mATX, and ATX from smallest to largest. The size will determine the number of PCIe connectors, RAM slots, and storage connectors that are available on the motherboard. The size is not determinant of quality, although Mini-ITX motherboards typically cost more, as they are a premium product.
